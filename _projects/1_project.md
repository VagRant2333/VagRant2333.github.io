This project was completed in the Shanghai Innovation Institute Preparatory Program for Undergraduates (Mar. 2025 to Jul. 2025).

I focused on embodied robot learning from simulation to real-world deployment:

- Trained standing and walking policies for Unitree Go2 using PPO in Isaac Lab.
- Performed sim-to-real transfer and real-robot validation.
- Worked with a multidisciplinary team including full-time doctoral students.

The project strengthened my practical understanding of policy learning, robot control stability, and deployment constraints in physical environments.
